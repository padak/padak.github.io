📚 Tense Guide

Present Simple

Used for:
• Regular habits and routines
• General facts and truths
• Permanent situations
• Schedules and timetables
Signal words: always, usually, often, sometimes, rarely, never, every day/week/month, on Mondays
✓ I play tennis every week.
✓ She works in a bank.
✓ The sun rises in the east.

Present Continuous

Used for:
• Actions happening right now
• Temporary situations
• Changing situations
• Future arrangements
Signal words: now, right now, at the moment, currently, Look!, Listen!, this week/month
✓ I am studying English now.
✓ They are building a new bridge.
✓ We are meeting tomorrow.
